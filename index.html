Welcome to Cybex Prefixer Homepage

Introduction

Cybex Prefixer helps you add a prefix to a phone number when placing a phone call. This is particularly useful for users of prepaid call plans. Simply enter your prefix when entering the app the first time. Anytime you want the prefix to be added open Cybex Prefixer instead of the Contacts App. Your contact list will be shown in our app as usual and you can make any phone call with the prefix added automatically.

Cybex Prefixer right now only supports adding prefix to international call that start with "+" or "00"

Usage

Cybex Prefixer will not alter the actual phone number but instead adds the prefix during call time.

Usage :

  1. The first time the app is opened, it will allow you to enter your custom prefix.
  2. Once this is done, the next time the the App is opened, it will directly show you your contact list.
  3. Choose the contact you wish to call and select his phone number from the contact detail. Cybex Prefixer will automatically call the selected phone number with the added prefix.

Change the prefix:

  1. Open the app. The contact list is shown.
  2. Tap "Cancel" on the top left. The contact screen is dismissed and the settings screen is shown
  3. Enter the new prefix
  4. Click "Show Contacts again to place a call.
